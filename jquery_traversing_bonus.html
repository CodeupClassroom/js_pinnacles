jQuery Traversing

        Bonus:

        Create 3 divs that look like a picture Frame, each one has a unique background image, create a button under each image that swaps the image from the frame. Use Traversing functions of jQuery.

        The rules are the following:

        The left frame swaps to the right and takes the image from the frame in the center.
        The center frame swaps randomly one of the images left or right.
        The right frame swaps to the left and takes the image from the frame in the center.
